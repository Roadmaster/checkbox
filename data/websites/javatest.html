Can you play the game?



Play Instructions:
Click the Applet once to start.
Use Left/Right Arrow keys to aim the launcher.
Use Shift or Up Arrow to fire.
M toggles color blind mode (bubbles gain symbols to help differentiate them.)
P to pause.
S to toggle sound On/Off.

When you've had enough fun, please close the browser.
